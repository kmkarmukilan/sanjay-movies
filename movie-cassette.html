The Story

About the Archive

The Cinema Archive is a passion project born from a love of celluloid and the warmth of magnetic tape. We believe the greatest films deserve to be held — not just streamed.

Each cassette in our vault is hand-selected for its cultural significance, artistic merit, and its enduring power to move an audience. Prices are in Indian Rupees (₹). All titles ship within India.
